<p><p>Additive Manufacturing Systems encompass two primary market types: Polymer and Metal. Polymer Additive Manufacturing Systems utilize thermoplastics and resins to create lightweight, customizable parts, commonly used in industries like aerospace and automotive for rapid prototyping and end-use applications. In contrast, Metal Additive Manufacturing Systems employ methods like selective laser melting to produce intricate metal components with superior strength and durability, catering to sectors such as healthcare and tooling. Both systems enhance production efficiency and design flexibility in modern manufacturing.</p></p>

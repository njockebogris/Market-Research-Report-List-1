<p><p>The automotive windshield washer pump market includes two main types: Mono Pump and Dual Pump. Mono Pumps are single-unit systems designed to supply washer fluid to either the front or rear windshield, offering simplicity and cost-effectiveness. Dual Pumps, on the other hand, feature two separate units or a single unit with dual outlets, enabling simultaneous or independent cleaning of both front and rear windshields. Dual Pumps provide enhanced functionality and convenience, catering to vehicles with advanced cleaning needs, while Mono Pumps are ideal for basic applications.</p></p>

<p><p>The polyurethane insulation material market is categorized into three main types: flexible foam, rigid foam, and spray foam. Flexible foam is lightweight and offers excellent cushioning, making it suitable for applications like furniture and automotive interiors. Rigid foam provides superior thermal insulation and structural support, commonly used in construction and cold storage. Spray foam, which expands upon application, creates air-tight seals and is ideal for irregular surfaces, enhancing energy efficiency in residential and commercial buildings.</p></p>

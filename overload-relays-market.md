<p><ul><li>Thermal Overload Relay</li><li>Electronic Overload Relays</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The overload relays market comprises primarily thermal and electronic overload relays. Thermal overload relays utilize a bimetallic strip that bends in response to heat generated by excessive current, providing protection by disconnecting the circuit. In contrast, electronic overload relays offer enhanced precision and features, such as adjustable settings and integrated fault diagnostic capabilities, using electronic components to monitor current. Both types are essential for safeguarding electric motors and equipment from damage due to overload conditions in various industrial applications.</p></p>

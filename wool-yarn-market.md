<p><p>Wool yarn is a natural fiber derived from the fleece of sheep and other animals like goats, alpacas, and rabbits. Additionally, advancements in wool processing technologies and the development of blended yarns have expanded its applications, further boosting market growth.</p><p>Key trends in the Wool Yarn Market include the rise of organic and ethically sourced wool, as consumers prioritize sustainability and animal welfare. The demand for premium and luxury wool products, such as merino wool, is also increasing. Emerging markets in Asia-Pacific and Latin America are expected to play a significant role in driving future growth.</p></p>

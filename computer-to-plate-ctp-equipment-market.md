<p><p>The Computer to Plate (CTP) equipment market comprises several types based on plate technology. Thermo-sensitive plates utilize heat to create images, offering high precision and fast processing. Lithostar Ultra plates are designed for high-quality lithographic printing, providing excellent image reproduction. Photopolymer formes use light-sensitive materials that harden upon exposure, ideal for packaging and label printing. Other categories may include various specialized plates catering to diverse printing requirements, ensuring versatility and adaptability in the printing industry.</p></p>

<p><ul><li>Liquid Permanent Marker</li><li>Solid Permanent Marker</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The permanent marker market is primarily divided into two types: liquid permanent markers and solid permanent markers. Liquid permanent markers contain vibrant ink that is delivered through a felt tip, offering versatility for various surfaces like paper and plastic. They dry quickly and are often waterproof. In contrast, solid permanent markers, sometimes resembling crayons, use a solid ink form that can be applied directly to surfaces. Both types cater to different user needs, ranging from arts and crafts to industrial applications.</p></p>

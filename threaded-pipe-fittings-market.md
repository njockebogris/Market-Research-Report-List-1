<p><strong>The Threaded Pipe Fittings Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Internal Thread</li><li>External Thread</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Threaded pipe fittings are components used to connect pipes securely, featuring either internal (female) or external (male) threads. Internal thread fittings have threads on the inside, allowing them to connect to pipes or fittings with external threads. External thread fittings have threads on the outside, designed to fit into internal thread counterparts. Both types ensure leak-proof connections in plumbing, gas, and industrial systems, with the choice depending on the specific application and compatibility requirements.</p></p>

<p><p>The biodegradable nonwovens market includes **Drylaid Nonwovens**, made by mechanically or chemically bonding fibers without water, offering versatility and eco-friendliness. **Wetlaid Nonwovens** involve suspending fibers in water before forming a web, ideal for lightweight, high-strength applications. **Spunbond Nonwovens** are produced by extruding molten polymers into continuous filaments, creating durable, breathable materials. All three types prioritize sustainability, using biodegradable materials like PLA or natural fibers, catering to industries like hygiene, agriculture, and packaging while reducing environmental impact.</p></p>

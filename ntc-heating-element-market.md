SMD types are designed for compact, high-density applications, allowing for easy integration onto circuit boards, enhancing thermal management in limited spaces. In contrast, Through Hole types are utilized in applications requiring stronger mechanical connections and are suited for more robust environments. Both types serve diverse industries, including automotive, consumer electronics, and medical devices, catering to distinct thermal control needs.</p></p>

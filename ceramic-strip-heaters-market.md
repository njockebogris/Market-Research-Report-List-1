<p><p>The ceramic strip heater market consists of two main types: Radiant Ceramic Strip Heaters (PCTR) and Rigid Ceramic Strip Heaters (PCR). PCTR heaters emit radiant heat efficiently, ideal for heating surfaces or environments quickly. They are commonly used in industrial applications, such as drying and curing processes. On the other hand, PCR heaters provide flexible heating solutions, often featuring a robust design for integration into various machinery, enhancing durability and thermal performance in manufacturing settings.</p></p>

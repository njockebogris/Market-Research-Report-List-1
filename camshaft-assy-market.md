<p><p>The Camshaft Assembly market primarily comprises three types: Overhead Camshaft (OHC), One-piece Camshaft (OPC), and Others. OHC designs feature camshafts positioned above the cylinder head, enhancing efficiency and response in modern engines. OPC, a consolidated design, offers simplicity and durability, often found in older vehicles. The "Others" category includes specialized or hybrid camshaft systems catering to niche applications. Each type plays a crucial role in optimizing engine performance, contributing to fuel efficiency and emissions control in various automotive sectors.</p></p>

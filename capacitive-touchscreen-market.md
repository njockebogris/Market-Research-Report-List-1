<p><p>Capacitive touchscreen technology primarily includes several types, each catering to specific applications and functionalities. The ATT (All-Transparent-Technology) type features a fully transparent conductive layer for optimal visibility and touch sensitivity, ideal for high-end devices. The G/G (Glass-on-Glass) type uses two layers of glass for enhanced durability and sensitivity, common in smartphones and tablets. Other types include projected capacitive touchscreens that utilize a grid of sensors, offering a balance between performance and cost for various consumer electronics.</p></p>

<p><p>The diesel generator market is primarily segmented into air-cooled and water-cooled generators. Air-cooled diesel generators use ambient air to dissipate heat, making them suitable for smaller applications and environments with limited water supply. They are typically more compact and easier to maintain. In contrast, water-cooled diesel generators utilize a liquid coolant for enhanced thermal efficiency, allowing for larger power outputs and extended runtime. These generators are often preferred in industrial and commercial settings where higher performance and durability are required.</p></p>

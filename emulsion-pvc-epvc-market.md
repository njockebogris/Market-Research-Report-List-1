<p><p>Emulsion PVC (EPVC) is a type of PVC produced through emulsion polymerization, offering fine particle size and high purity. The market is divided into **Homogeneous Type** and **Heterogeneous Type**. Homogeneous EPVC has uniform particle distribution, ideal for applications requiring consistency, such as coatings and adhesives. Heterogeneous EPVC has varied particle sizes, making it suitable for flexible products like flooring and synthetic leather. Both types cater to diverse industrial needs, with homogeneous focusing on precision and heterogeneous on versatility.</p></p>

<p><ul><li>Forearm Crutch</li><li>Underarm Or Axilla Crutch</li><li>Platform Crutch</li><li>Leg Support Crutch</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Disabled Crutches market comprises several types, each catering to specific mobility needs. Forearm crutches, also known as elbow crutches, provide support through the forearm for enhanced stability. Underarm or axilla crutches are the traditional crutches used for weight bearing under the arms. Platform crutches feature a horizontal support for the forearm, allowing for greater weight distribution. Lastly, leg support crutches offer assistance while keeping the legs elevated, ideal for users with lower limb injuries or disabilities, enhancing mobility and comfort.</p></p>

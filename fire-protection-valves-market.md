<p><strong>The Fire Protection Valves Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Check Valves</li><li>Butterfly Valves</li><li>Gate Valves</li><li>Ball Valves</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fire protection valves market encompasses several types essential for efficient firefighting systems. Check valves prevent backflow, ensuring water only moves in one direction. Butterfly valves regulate flow with a rotating disc, offering quick operation. Gate valves provide straightforward on/off control, ideal for shut-off applications. Ball valves utilize a spherical closure element, allowing for rapid flow control with minimal resistance. Other types include pressure relief and deluge valves, which cater to specific fire protection needs, enhancing safety and system reliability.</p></p>

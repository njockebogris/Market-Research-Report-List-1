<p><strong>The Sunscreen Cream Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Chemical Sunscreens</li><li>Physical Sunscreens</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The sunscreen cream market is primarily divided into two types: chemical sunscreens and physical sunscreens. Chemical sunscreens contain organic compounds that absorb UV radiation, converting it into heat, which is then released from the skin. They typically offer a lightweight feel and are easily absorbed. In contrast, physical sunscreens contain mineral ingredients like zinc oxide or titanium dioxide that act as a physical barrier, reflecting and scattering UV rays. They are often recommended for sensitive skin and provide immediate sun protection.</p></p>
